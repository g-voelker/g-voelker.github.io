The freely propagating Lagrangian ray volumes describing the wave may be distributed randomly in phase space (Fig. 3). To derive the mean-flow impact of the waves one may integrate over all wave numbers and then estimate the wave action at the desired location.

Here we implement the procedure into the finite volume model UA-ICON and therefore require cell averaged flux divergences or equivalently the fluxes across the cell boundaries. To estimate the latter (say at height \(z_n\), compare Fig. 3) and at the same time reduce the model noise we average the wave fluxes, that is integrate the corresponding ray volume properties, over the staggered cell around the cell boundary at hand (in this case \(z\in[z_{n-\frac{1}{2}}, z_{n+\frac{1}{2}}]\)).
